ABOUT US

ZeroHunger is the United Nations' second Sustainable Development Goal, aiming to eradicate hunger, ensure food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture by 2030. It focuses on providing everyone, especially the poor and vulnerable, with sufficient, safe, and nutritious food all year round, while also supporting sustainable farming practices.

OUR MISSION AND VISION

To develop an innovative web platform that combats hunger by connecting communities, providing resources, and promoting sustainable agriculture. We envision a world where everyone has access to nutritious food, empowered by technology and community collaboration, to achieve Zero Hunger, ensuring food security and improved nutrition for all.
